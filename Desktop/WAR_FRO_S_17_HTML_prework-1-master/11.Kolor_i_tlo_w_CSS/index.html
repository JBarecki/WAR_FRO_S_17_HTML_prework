The Chocolate Bhutlah is a strain of chile pepper, originally created by Chad Soleski. The variety was made by accident, via an unintentional cross-pollination. The mother plant was the Red Bhutlah, which is a cross between the Trinidad Douglah, and Bhut Jolokia. Chad Soleski gave those cross-pollinated seeds for the Red Bhutlah to Steven McLaurin, ho grew them out and discover the chocolate colored pods.
